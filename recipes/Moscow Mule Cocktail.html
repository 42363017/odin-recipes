Thanksgiving Dinner Drink


This classic Moscow mule recipe uses vodka, ginger beer, ice, and lime to create a refreshing cocktail that has nothing to do with Moscow or mules

A Moscow mule is a vodka-based cocktail made with lime juice and ginger beer. Though the drink is associated with Russia (for obvious reasons), it's actually an American invention that dates back to the 1940s.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ fluid ounces vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce lime juice
  • ice cubes
  • ½ cup ginger beer
  • 1 lime wedge for garnish

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug or glass; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine. Drop a lime wedge into the mug or glass for garnish.
